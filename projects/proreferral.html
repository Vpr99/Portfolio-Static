The Pro is the Show

I was responsible for the ‘Pro’ part of Pro Referral. Simplicity was the encompassing theme in designing the experience, since Pros want to spend as little time possible managing their jobs - and more time out doing them.

The Dashboard

When a Pro launches the app, they are greeted by a high-level view of everything new with their business. It shows any new job requests that they've received since last vising the app, as well as any unread activity from customers.

Lead Triage

When a Pro receives a new lead, they can quickly evaluate whether it's something they're interested pursuing. From there, they can either pass or follow up with the customer.

Messaging

Pros can hold a conversation with the consumer, quickly reference job details and keep track of the job's status through the messaging screen. They can also schedule appointments and set reminders when an appointment is approaching.

Style Guide / UI Kit

As part of building the experience on web and app, I created a UI toolkit for designers and helped build the corresponding living style guide for web. The guide includes typography, UI components, iconography, colors and boilerplate code for easings / microinteractions. It is now powering the Pro experience and is being rolled out on the Consumer experience as well.
